About Us

We are a group of high school and college students from the Cleveland area with a passion for programming! We wanted to have a network of other students who share our interests and live in our community, so we started the Northeast Ohio Coding Coalition. Our hope is that we can make a welcoming community where we can learn and connect with each other in a great environment.
